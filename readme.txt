=== Gallery ===

Based on Underscores and built with Foundation, The Gallery is an easy to use theme for Worpress and is perfect for Artists, Galleries, Event Spaces and Organizations. Head over to stafford-creative.com/the-gallery to see final product and additional info about setup. Enjoy!
